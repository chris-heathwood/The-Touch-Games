# Privacy Policy

**The Touch Games**
Last updated: May 2026

## Overview

The Touch Games does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Game Center

The Touch Games uses Apple Game Center to submit scores to leaderboards. Game Center is provided by Apple Inc. and is subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not have access to any Game Center data beyond what is displayed within the app.

## Data Collection

We do not collect:
- Personal information
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports

## Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties.
