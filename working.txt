    - Event details
        - Beeper
            - Drag finger between two points
            - Faster and faster
            - Until you don't make it
            - Threshold based on the timing
            - Not too early, not too late
        - Tapper
            - 100 taps
            - Time between two spots (close together)
        - Swiper
            - Sprint Swiper
                - 100 swipes
                - Time between two spots (far apart together)
            - Middle distance Swiper
                - 4 corners
                - Swipe between
                - 400 swipes
            - Marathon Swiper
                - 4 corners
                - Swipe between
                - 40000 swipes
        - Rotator
            - Rotate 5 times
            - Get faster
            - Release between the arcs
        - Timer
            - 5 random beats to hit
            - 5th time hold and hit fast marker
        - Balancer
            - 2 fingers
            - Drag back
            - Keep circle balanced for as long as possible
        - Tracer
            - Follow a track
